03 — OOSINT · The Outlier Open-Source Intelligence Network

Open-source intelligence, with an outlier's eye, run as a network.

OSINT is the discipline of producing genuine intelligence from sources anyone can legally access: public records, reporting, data, imagery, research. No secrets. No intrusion. Only the signal already in the open, properly understood. OOSINT adds an outlier's eye for the data point others dismiss as noise — and runs it as a distributed body of minds.

O — Outlier
An outlier's eye for signal
Built to notice the anomaly that quietly says the prevailing story is wrong.
O — Open-Source
Public sources, open methods
Everything drawn from legally accessible information, with methods that can be audited. Closed intelligence rots; open intelligence compounds.
S/I — Intelligence
Understanding, not data
Raw information is not intelligence. Intelligence is structured, corroborated, contextualized — usable by someone who must act.
N — Network
A distributed body of minds
Not a desk — a curated community of researchers, AI-paired and outlier-first, paid to look.
Collect→ Connect→ Corroborate→ Contextualize→ Communicate
Privacy & ethics first. No surveillance of individuals. No facial scraping. No harm. OOSINT studies systems and public signal — and ships every claim with its certainty and its sources. We never launder a guess into a fact. Auditability is the feature.
